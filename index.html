ABOUT BUILDER HARDWARE

Building Excellence Since 1976

For over four decades, India International House Ltd. has been manufacturing architectural hardware and precision components with the same commitment to durability and design. From intricately crafted door handles and hinges to custom-engineered brackets, housings, and extruded profiles, our products are trusted by leading brands, OEMs, and project partners worldwide.

With vertically integrated manufacturing, skilled craftsmanship, and strict quality systems, we deliver reliable solutions that perform consistently—batch after batch, shipment after shipment.

Responsible Manufacturing

Our operations are designed to minimize impact and maximize efficiency:

Eco-friendly surface care systems reduce chemical use and conserve water.

Effluent treatment plants across all units ensure safe treatment and discharge of wastewater.

Induction furnaces lower energy consumption and emissions compared to conventional methods.

Trivalent chrome facilities provide a safer, more environmentally responsible plating process.

Sustainable packaging is developed to minimize material waste and support recyclability.

Certified & Audit-Ready

Our facilities and processes meet international quality and compliance standards:

  • ISO 9001:2015 — Quality Management
  • ISO 14001:2015 — Environmental Management
  • ISO 45001:2018 — Occupational Health & Safety
  • RoHS Compliant — Safe materials & finishes
  • SMETA / C-TPAT — Ethical trade & secure supply chains

OUR SERVICES

Delivering Excellence in Every Detail

With decades of expertise and advanced manufacturing capabilities, Builder Hardware offers a complete range of solutions to meet the needs of architects, builders, and designers worldwide.

Strict Quality Assurance

Every part undergoes salt-spray, thermal/humidity, adhesion, and color-matching tests, backed by ISO 9001/14001/45001 and RoHS compliance.

In-House Manufacturing

Tooling, forming, machining, finishing, and packaging under one roof—ensuring tighter control, faster sampling, and consistent delivery.

Premium Finishes

From polished brass to advanced PVD coatings, we offer a wide spectrum of finishes designed for durability, corrosion resistance, and long-term color stability.

Global Supply Network

Our export logistics expertise and packaging solutions enable dependable, on-time deliveries to OEMs and partners in 60+ countries.

Precision Manufacturing & Reliable Service

  • For decades, IIHL has delivered architectural hardware and precision components built with tight tolerances, advanced finishes, and dependable service. Our in-house forging, die-casting, CNC/VMC machining, extrusion, and finishing capabilities allow us to meet demanding customer requirements across global markets.

Every project is backed by:

  • Documented tolerances and strict QC protocols
  • Surface finish and durability testing (salt-spray, adhesion, thermal, color)
  • Export packaging designed for protection and recyclability
  • Straightness of 0.25mm/meter (0.0010″ per foot T.I.R).
  • Being India’s largest producer of these high-precision bars, we can supply special long-length precision bars in lengths up to 11.5 meters (37 feet long). This allows us to meet specific customer requests for non-standard PSQ requirements on an expedited basis.
  • Our PSQ / shafting bars are more than 8 times straighter than industry standards (ASTM A108), with straightness readings of 0.0015” T.I.R per ft max.
  • No one else enjoys producing the types of stainless steel bars that we like doing!
