ABOUT Suri Products (India)

Headquartered in Delhi, SP is a leading global components manufacturer and supplier for Tier 1 companies and the independent aftermarket. Established in 1966, Suri Products (India) has emerged as a distinguished leader in the automotive industry, specializing in the manufacturing of hydraulic brake parts, rubber-to-metal bonded parts, and precision rubber components. With decades of expertise, the company has built a strong reputation for quality, innovation, and reliability across both Indian and international markets.

SP offers a comprehensive range of components for heavy-duty commercial vehicles, trailers, earthmoving equipment, material handling, and agricultural vehicles, catering to Indian, European, Japanese, Chinese, and American applications.
