About Us

Established in 1976, our company began as a family-run enterprise built on trust, integrity, and craftsmanship. Over the years, we have transformed into a global manufacturer of architectural hardware and allied solutions, while preserving the values that shaped our foundation. Today, we combine traditional expertise with modern innovation to deliver world-class products to customers across the globe.

Quality & Compliance

We uphold the highest standards of product quality, safety, and compliance, ensuring every product meets international benchmarks. Our facilities are equipped with advanced testing and inspection processes, including:

  1. Salt Spray Testing – for corrosion resistance and durability
  2. Thermal & Humidity Testing – to ensure performance in diverse environments
  3. Adhesion & Color Matching Tests – for finish consistency and aesthetics
  4. ROHS Compliance – use of environmentally safe and compliant materials
  5. ISO Certifications – ISO 9001:2015, ISO 14001:2015 & ISO 45001:2018 standards
