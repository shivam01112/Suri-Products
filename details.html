L'Enfant Solid Brass Small Backplate

Description

L'Enfant Solid Brass Small Backplate offers a sleek, modern design ideal for contemporary interiors. Crafted from durable, high-quality materials, this push plate provides a seamless blend of style and functionality.

Perfect for residential and commercial use, the L'Enfant Solid Brass Small Backplate enhances both the look and durability of your doors.

Available Dimensions Length - 11”, Width - 2.7”
Available Materials Brass, Aluminium, Stainless Steel and Copper.
Available Finishes Satin Brush, Polished Brass Lacquered, Chrome Plated, Satin Nickel, Antique Brass, Antique Pewter, Satin Brass, Oil Rubbed Brass (Orb) and more.
Cleaning Use warm water and mild soap. Avoid using abrasives.
