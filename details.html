About Suri Products (India)

Headquartered in Delhi, Suri Products (India) is a leading manufacturer and supplier of high-quality automotive components for Tier 1 companies and the independent aftermarket. Established in 1966, SP specializes in hydraulic brake parts, rubber-to-metal bonded parts, and precision rubber components.

Serving both Indian and international markets, SP offers components for heavy-duty commercial vehicles, trailers, earthmoving equipment, material handling, and agricultural vehicles. The company is renowned for its quality, innovation, and reliability.
